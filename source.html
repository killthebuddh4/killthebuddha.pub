source

Rick Rubin talks a lot about Source in his new book. Source is the thing that is, the thing that artists communicate. I'm a little bit different, maybe. For me, Source is the Reason. For me, Source is the world and other people. For me, Source is Life, or the Good Life, or something like that. Maybe that's the same Source, I guess.

One of the more important lessons from my childhood is that unhappy people don't do well. I have a hard time connecting with Source, and I'm kind of an unhappy fellow. But I know that, whatever I do, if I don't show my children the face of happiness, if I don't expose them to the Source, I'll have failed them.
