kill the buddha

image

The image of a function is its output. I've always found this technical term to be quite beautiful. The function is a lens, static and frictionless, pure existence, and the universe is a flow through it. There's no flowing, just flow, there's just a universe, a lens, and an image. To me the urge to write things down feels like the urge to just be a lens. I don't want to do anything, I don't want to have a purpose or a goal or affectation, I just want to be a lens with an image, and I want to be empty.
