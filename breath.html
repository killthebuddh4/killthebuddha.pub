kill the buddha

breath

Of all the Silicon Valley idols, I hate productivity the most. I don't want to be productive, I want to breathe. Life feels chaotic but it unfolds in the straightest line. Life doesn't fork nor branch nor meander, it just goes. "What shall I do next?" is the only question you (or Bill Gates, Leonardo da Vinci, or the Buddha, or anyone) has ever answered. You don't choose a path, alternatives don't exist. The only air you've ever breathed is the air right in front of you.
